SMS Program Terms

LoanX enables loan officers to communicate with borrowers via SMS regarding their mortgage loan. Below are the details of our messaging program.

What Messages Will I Receive?

Your loan officer may send you SMS messages related to your mortgage loan, including status updates, document requests, and general loan communications. Message frequency varies based on your loan activity.

How Do I Opt In?

After providing your phone number during the loan application process, you will receive an initial SMS asking for your consent. Reply with one of the following keywords to opt in:

  • YES
  • START
  • SUBSCRIBE
"LoanX: Your loan officer would like to message you about your loan. Reply YES to opt in. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out."

How Do I Opt Out?

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent. Reply START to resubscribe.

Need Help?

Reply HELP to any message for support information, or contact us at support@loanx.ai.

Rates & Carriers

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
